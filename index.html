The Muslim Students' Society of Nigeria (MSSN), established in 1954, aims to safeguard the interests of Muslims in educational institutions across Nigeria.
The MSSN-LASU Branch, a religious and social group of Muslim students on campus, has been an integral part of the university community since its inception in 1984..
